Frequently Asked Questions

What is web hosting?

Web hosting is a service that allows individuals and organizations to make their websites accessible on the internet. Hosting providers store website files on servers, making it possible for users to access the site by entering the domain name.

What types of hosting plans do you offer?

We offer various hosting plans to meet different needs, including shared hosting, VPS hosting, dedicated hosting, and cloud hosting. Each plan comes with unique features and resources.

What is shared hosting?

Shared hosting is a hosting environment where multiple websites share resources on the same server. It's a cost-effective option suitable for small to medium-sized websites with moderate traffic.

Is shared hosting suitable for my business?

Shared hosting is ideal for small to medium-sized businesses, blogs, and personal websites. If your website grows, you can easily upgrade to a more robust hosting plan.

Can I host multiple websites on a shared hosting plan?

Yes, many of our shared hosting plans allow you to host multiple websites under a single account. Check our plans for specific details.

How is VPS hosting different from shared hosting?

In VPS hosting, your website gets dedicated resources, including CPU, RAM, and storage. This provides better performance and scalability compared to shared hosting, where resources are shared among multiple websites.

Can I customize my VPS hosting plan??

Yes, VPS hosting allows for greater customization. You can choose the amount of RAM, storage, and other resources based on your website's specific needs.
